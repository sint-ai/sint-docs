## Why this material is archived

Over 2024 and 2025, SINT shipped in two directions simultaneously: an open-source governance protocol for AI agents acting in the physical world, and a consumer Web3 agent platform with a $SINT token, Telegram Mini App, and gamified staking mechanics.

In April 2026, SINT Labs refocused. Physical AI governance — the Protocol, the products, the Studio work — is where we can build the biggest, most durable company. The Web3 consumer work is paused.

## What "archived" means here

- **Nothing is being sunset.** Existing commitments stand.
- **The material is preserved.** Partners and community members who need to reference the original whitepaper, partnership announcements, or platform designs can find them in this archive.
- **The work is paused, not deleted.** We may return to consumer Web3 agent work in the future. The door stays open.
- **SINT Labs' current focus is elsewhere.** Protocol, products, Studio. Not token economics, not TMA growth, not staking mechanics.
