## Overview

Between 2024 and 2025, SINT operated a consumer-facing AI agent platform with Web3 characteristics:

- **SINT Hive** — the brand for the consumer product
- **Telegram Mini App** — the primary client
- **$SINT token** — utility and governance token
- **60,000+ users** — peak onboarding across the Telegram funnel
- **Agent marketplace** — with Proof of Reputation scoring
- **Gamified AI builder** — quest-based rewards for training synthetic intelligence agents

## Why it's archived

SINT Labs refocused in April 2026 on physical-AI governance (Protocol, Products, Studio). Consumer Web3 is a different business with different economics, different users, and different go-to-market requirements. Running both under one brand was creating friction with both audiences.

## Will the Web3 work return?

Possibly. The Web3 consumer work is paused, not canceled. If SINT Labs revisits this direction, it will be under a clear, separate brand with a dedicated announcement.
