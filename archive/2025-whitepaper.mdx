## What this whitepaper describes

The March 2025 SINT whitepaper positioned SINT as a decentralized agentic AI ecosystem with five interconnected components:

1. **SINT Core** — a "synthetic intelligence" AI agent framework
2. **SINT Launchpad & Marketplace** — token-based marketplace for agents with Proof of Reputation
3. **Mini App** — Telegram-native client for agent interaction
4. **DeAi Layer** — decentralized AI infrastructure integrated with Fetch.ai, SophiaVerse, SingularityNET, and Cosmos SDK
5. **Robotics Framework** — gamified physical-AI training and deployment

The paper introduced the $SINT token as the ecosystem's utility and governance asset, with tokenomics covering staking, marketplace fees, Proof of Reputation rewards, and developer incentives.
