## ICE Open Network (September 2025)

SINT announced integration with ICE Open Network's Online+ layer in September 2025. The partnership focused on AI-enhanced Web3 apps, payments, gaming, and social engagement on the ICE ecosystem.

**Status as of April 2026:** the announcement happened. The partnership exists in the historical record. SINT Labs' current focus is on SINT Protocol, Products, and Studio — none of which overlaps with the ICE integration use case. If ICE Open Network partners wish to discuss the relationship going forward, contact i@pshkv.com.

## Ozak AI (2025)

Ozak AI and SINT announced a collaboration around 30ms market signals flowing through SINT's agent stack. The use case targeted automated trading and Web3 user experience.

**Status as of April 2026:** similar to the ICE partnership — the announcement is part of the historical record. It does not describe current SINT Labs work.

## Infrastructure references in the 2025 whitepaper

The March 2025 whitepaper referenced several Web3 infrastructure projects as context or aspirational partners:

- **Fetch.ai** — for agent communication framework
- **SophiaVerse** — for agent training scenarios
- **SingularityNET** — for decentralized AI marketplace concepts
- **Cosmos SDK** — for the proposed DeAi Layer

These references described a roadmap direction, not signed engineering integrations. Current SINT Protocol and products do not depend on any of these projects.

## What counts as a current SINT Labs partnership

For avoidance of confusion: current SINT Labs partnerships are listed on [sint.gg](https://sint.gg) directly, not in this archive. If a partner is not listed on the current site, that partnership should be treated as historical rather than active.
