# Support

## If you're a SINT Labs client

Your engagement has a direct Slack Connect channel and a named point of contact. Use those first — response times are committed in your SOW.

Incidents:

- **Severity 1** (production outage, data integrity concern, E-stop not clearing): page via the on-call number in your runbook
- **Severity 2** (degraded service, approval delays > SLA): Slack Connect
- **Severity 3** (questions, small requests): Slack Connect or email

## If you're a Protocol contributor or self-hosted user

- **Bugs:** [github.com/sint-ai/sint-protocol/issues](https://github.com/sint-ai/sint-protocol/issues)
- **Questions:** [github.com/sint-ai/sint-protocol/discussions](https://github.com/sint-ai/sint-protocol/discussions)
- **Security disclosures:** security@sint.gg (see [Security policy](/protocol/security))

## If you're evaluating SINT for your company

**[sint.gg/contact](https://sint.gg/contact)** for the conversation. Two-week fixed-fee Audit is the first step; we don't do unpaid discovery marathons.

## If you're press, analyst, or investor

press@sint.gg · [press kit](/press) · illia@sint.gg for founder availability.

## Docs issues

Any page has a "Suggest edit" link at the bottom. PRs against [github.com/sint-ai/sint-docs](https://github.com/sint-ai/sint-docs) are welcome.
