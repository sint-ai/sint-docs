# Revenue Operator

**One-liner:** Outbound, follow-up, and pipeline hygiene executed end-to-end, under audit.

## What it replaces

- SDR seats running sequence tools (Apollo, Outreach, Salesloft)
- RevOps analyst time on Salesforce/HubSpot pipeline hygiene
- Manual quote-to-cash handoff between sales and finance

## Safety envelope

| Tier | Action classes |
|---|---|
| T0 | `intake.linkedin.read`, `intake.crm.read`, `score.lead` |
| T1 | `draft.outbound`, `send.outbound` (templated) |
| T2 | `send.outbound` (ad-hoc), `reschedule.followup` |
| T3 | `crm.opportunity.create`, `crm.contact.create` |
| T4 | `pricing.reference`, anything that mentions a discount |

Hard limits (encoded in the capability token, not in config):

- Rate cap per recipient domain
- Domain exclusion list (compliance-maintained)
- Legal-review gate on any jurisdiction flagged by Gate
- Spend cap on outbound LLM calls (per [Economy](/protocol/layers/economy) meter)

## Metrics

- Replies per 1k touches
- Meetings booked
- Cost per meeting (LLM + tool spend / meetings)
- Pipeline hygiene score (CRM completeness rubric)
- Hours reclaimed (vs prior-state SDR baseline)

## Pricing band

$8k–$18k/mo retainer + 5–10% outcome share on net-new pipeline. Illustrative; scoped during the [Audit](https://sint.gg/agency).
