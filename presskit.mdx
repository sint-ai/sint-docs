## One-liner

SINT Labs is an AI research and engineering lab building the open-source governance protocol for autonomous systems — and the products and services that run on it.

## One paragraph

SINT Labs builds the authorization layer for AI agents that act in the physical or economic world. Our SINT Protocol is an open-source capability-based governance standard with twelve transport bridges, four approval tiers, a SHA-256 hash-chained evidence ledger, and compliance mappings to IEC 62443, the EU AI Act, and the NIST AI RMF. We ship products on the protocol (Console, Avatars, Operators, CMO Operator — unified as SINT OS), and we deploy the full stack for enterprise clients through our Studio practice. Founded in 2024 by Illia Pashkov, headquartered in Los Angeles.

## Brand usage

"SINT Labs" should be written as two words, both capitalized.

Do not use:
- "Sint Labs" (lowercase "int")
- "SINT-Labs" (hyphen)
- "SINTLabs" (no space)

Correct: **SINT Labs**.

Product names:
- **SINT Protocol** — the governance protocol
- **SINT OS** — the runtime (note: "OS" is uppercase, no period)
- **SINT Console** — the operator UI
- **SINT Avatars** — the avatar product (plural, even when referring to single instance)
- **SINT Operators** — the automation product
- **CMO Operator** — the content product (no "SINT" prefix by convention)
- **SINT Studio** — the services arm
