# Press kit

## Company

**SINT Labs** — incorporated as PSHKV Inc., Los Angeles, California.

Founded 2024; rebranded and refocused in early 2026. See [/origin](/origin) for the pivot narrative.

## What we do

Two products:

1. **SINT Protocol** — open-source (Apache 2.0) 5-layer governance standard for AI agents acting in the physical and financial world. [github.com/sint-ai/sint-protocol](https://github.com/sint-ai/sint-protocol).
2. **SINT Operators** — custom autonomous workflow systems built and run for US mid-market services firms ($3M–$20M revenue, 15–80 employees).

Supporting products: **SINT Operator Platform** (Console, Commons, Memory, Cron, Canon, Pipelines), **SINT Face** (voice-first 3D interface).
