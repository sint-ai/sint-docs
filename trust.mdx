# Trust

A plain-English version of how we operate.

## Data

- **Your data stays yours.** We are custodians, not owners. Contractual defaults include export on request in open formats and deletion-on-termination clauses.
- **Per-tenant isolation.** Your data is logically and physically isolated from other tenants. Shared infrastructure (the SOP control plane) never commingles tenant data.
- **PII minimization.** Operators redact PII before it reaches LLM prompts where the LLM call doesn't require the PII. Per-tenant configuration controls what counts as PII.
- **Retention is yours to set.** Minimums apply for regulated workflows (HIPAA, financial). Maxima are yours to set.

## Systems

- **Least privilege on every integration.** If an Operator needs to read HubSpot contacts, its token permits exactly that scope — not "everything HubSpot."
- **Token revocation is instant.** <50ms p99 across three regions. Revoking an Operator's access means immediate, not "next time the cache refreshes."
- **Hardware E-stop universality.** For Operators touching physical systems, the E-stop is unconditional and bypasses every token check.
- **No write to a system not in the Pipeline's declared `outputs`.** Drift between declaration and runtime is a deploy blocker, not a warning.

## Work

- **Every decision is ledgered.** Append-only, hash-chained, continuously verified. If it isn't in the ledger, it didn't happen.
- **Every T2+ action requires human approval.** No "trusted agent" exception.
- **Weekly reports.** You see what the Operator did; you see what was escalated; you see what changed.
- **We dogfood.** SINT Labs runs on its own Operators and on an internal OpenClaw orchestration setup. When SINT is broken, we're the first to feel it.

## Certifications

We do not currently hold SOC 2, ISO 27001, or HITRUST. For regulated workflows requiring formal certifications, we scope a certification path as part of the engagement. We will not claim certifications we don't hold.

## What we won't do

- Sell your data. Ever.
- Train a shared model on your data across tenants.
- Take actions on your systems that aren't declared in the Pipeline and token-scoped.
- Ship an Operator that skips the ledger.
- Deploy to production without [M8 Guardrails](/platform/overview#m8-guardrails) passing.

## Incidents

If something goes wrong, you will hear it from us first — clearly, with a timeline, with a root cause, with remediation. We would rather tell you what happened than protect our narrative.
